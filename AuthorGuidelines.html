For Authors

Author Guidelines

Follow these requirements to ensure smooth review and publication.

Manuscript Preparation

  • Language: English (clear and concise).
  • File format: DOCX or LaTeX-generated PDF.
  • Include title, abstract, keywords, and references.
  • Figures/tables must be high resolution with captions.
  • Use consistent citation style throughout manuscript.

Required Documents

  • Cover letter signed by corresponding author.
  • Declaration of originality and no simultaneous submission.
  • Conflict of interest and funding statements.
  • Ethical approval document where applicable.
