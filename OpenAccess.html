Journal Policies

Open Access Policy

All published papers are freely available online with immediate open access.

Access and Licensing

Articles are accessible without subscription barriers and can be read, downloaded, and shared for academic use with proper attribution.

  • Immediate online availability after publication.
  • No embargo period.
  • Long-term archiving for preservation.

Author Rights

Authors retain copyright and grant the journal non-exclusive publishing rights.

Re-use permissions are granted under journal licensing terms with proper citation.

IJSRI

International Journal of Scientific Research & Innovation is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across all scientific disciplines.
