Deep Learning Approaches for Medical Image Classification: A Comprehensive Survey

Authors: A. Jain, Lorenzo, Marcel Chen, David Wilson

Abstract

This paper presents a comprehensive survey of deep learning techniques applied to medical image diagnostics. We review convolutional neural networks, transfer learning methods, and recent architectures validated over CT and MRI datasets.

Keywords

Deep Learning, Medical Imaging, CNN, Diagnostic AI, Transfer Learning
