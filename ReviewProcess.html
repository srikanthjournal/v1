Review Process

IJSRI follows a transparent and timely double-blind peer-review workflow.

1. Initial Screening

Editorial check for scope, quality, plagiarism, and formatting compliance.

2. Peer Review

Two or more experts evaluate novelty, methodology, and scientific merit.

3. Decision

Accept, minor revision, major revision, or reject with reviewer feedback.

Typical Timeline

Stage Time
Initial Editorial Check 3-5 days
Peer Review Round 2-3 weeks
Author Revision 1-2 weeks
Final Decision and Publication Within 1 week
