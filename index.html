ABOUT THE JOURNAL

Publishing Excellence in Scientific Research

The International Journal of Scientific Research & Innovation (IJSRI) is an international, peer-reviewed, open-access journal with the objective to explore and publish knowledge across scientific disciplines.

We provide a highly reachable platform for researchers, practitioners, and academics worldwide.

  • Rigorous peer-review process
  • Fast online access to all articles
  • Indexed in major databases
  • Rapid publication within 2-3 weeks
  • DOI assigned to each article
  • Transparent editorial policy
